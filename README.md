DESIGNO – Project Overview

DESIGNO is a full‑service creative platform that brings together web design, mobile app development, and graphic design under one roof. The site is built to help businesses and individuals turn ideas into polished digital experiences, whether they need a sleek website, a custom app, or visual branding.

Key Features

- Service Showcase – Dedicated pages highlight web, app, and graphic design portfolios with live demos and case studies.
- Query Submission – A simple contact form lets visitors describe their project, upload files, and receive a quick turnaround quote.
- Client Dashboard – Registered users can track project status, exchange feedback, and access deliverables.
- Blog & Resources – Regular posts on design trends, tutorials, and best practices to engage the community.
- Responsive Design – The site is fully mobile‑friendly, ensuring a seamless experience across devices.

Goal

DESIGNO aims to become the go‑to hub for anyone seeking a cohesive design solution, streamlining the inquiry process and delivering high‑quality results that drive brand growth.
